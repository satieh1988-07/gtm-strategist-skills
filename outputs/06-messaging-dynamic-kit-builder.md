# Dynamic Kit Builder — Feature Messaging

**Date:** April 30, 2026  
**Feature:** Dynamic Kit Builder (Freedom Back Office)  
**Use:** Feature page copy, sales emails, release announcements, sales enablement

---

## Headline Options

1. **Let customers build exactly what they want — and buy more while they do it.**
2. **More revenue per order. Zero custom dev.**
3. **Turn your catalog into a guided shopping experience.**

**Recommended:** Option 1 — leads with customer benefit (personalization) and DSO outcome (higher AOV) in one line.

---

## Subheadline

> Dynamic Kit Builder gives your reps and customers a step-by-step experience to build personalized kits from your catalog — with conditional logic, built-in offers, and automatic pricing built in.

---

## Feature-Benefit Bullets

- **Increase sales & AOV** — Guide customers through a personalized build experience that naturally surfaces more products and drives larger orders.
- **Fixed or fully flexible bundles** — Support both preset kits and fully customizable builds, so you can run enrollment offers, seasonal collections, and open-catalog builds from one tool.
- **Conditional, step-by-step flows** — Trigger the next step based on what a customer selects — no decision fatigue, no abandoned carts.
- **Built-in promotions that convert** — Apply targeted offers directly inside the builder to boost engagement at the moment of selection.
- **Precision pricing per variant** — Set unique prices and overrides per variation — size, color, style — so every combination prices correctly without manual workarounds.
- **Unlimited product properties** — Configure any kit your catalog requires, no matter how complex, without touching your dev team.

## Short-Form Copy

**For email announcement or release note:**

> **Dynamic Kit Builder is live.**
> Let your reps and customers build their own kits from your catalog — step by step, with built-in offers and automatic pricing. More personalization. Higher order value. No dev work.

**For a sales conversation one-liner:**

> "It's a guided kit builder — customers pick their products step by step, and it automatically applies pricing and promotions as they go. Great for enrollment kits, party bundles, or any scenario where you want to upsell without a hard sell."

---

## What Not to Lead With

- ~~Shopify comparison~~ — this is a platform feature, not a platform battle
- ~~Technical specs~~ (conditional configurations, variant management) — these are proof points, not headlines
- ~~"Flexible bundling"~~ as a headline — too generic; lead with the outcome (AOV, personalization) not the mechanism
